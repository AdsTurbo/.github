# AdsTurbo

AdsTurbo builds AI workflows for video ad production: product video briefs, UGC scripts, ad variation planning, storyboards, AI actor workflows, localization, and post-production tools.

Our open-source projects focus on practical creative operations for marketers, DTC teams, agencies, and developers building ad automation workflows.

## Open-source focus

- Product-page-to-ad brief generation
- TikTok, Reels, and Shorts hook ideation
- UGC ad scripts and storyboard templates
- AI video ad workflow schemas
- Agent and API tooling for ad creative production
